# Security Remediation Package Checklist

- Confirm the adopted evidence pack and the security assessment tell the same story about exploitability, affected surface, and unresolved gaps.
- Confirm the selected remediation plan states what changes will be made, why that option was chosen, and what constraints must be respected during rollout.
- Confirm the verification plan defines concrete proof that the fix closes the finding and guards against regression.
- Confirm the rollout plan and rollback safety plan are consistent with deployment constraints and operational risk.
- Confirm the stakeholder communication draft is accurate, scoped to the known evidence, and does not overclaim closure.
- Confirm the closure evidence requirements make remaining proof obligations explicit instead of implying that publication alone proves remediation.
