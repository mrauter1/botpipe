# Incident Hardening Package Checklist

Use this checklist while assembling `incident_resolution_package.md`.

- Incident trigger, window, and affected system are explicit.
- Response objectives and incident boundary are restated clearly.
- Timeline and blast-radius highlights are summarized.
- Observability gaps and unresolved evidence gaps are explicit.
- Ranked hypotheses and immediate mitigations are reflected accurately.
- Hardening workstreams and proving milestones are explicit.
- Backlog items include priority and expected closure evidence.
- Follow-up owners or role expectations are visible.
- Stakeholder communications stay aligned to the assessed posture and confidence level.
